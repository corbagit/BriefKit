# BriefKit Onboarding Email Sequence: Content Summarizer

This document details the onboarding email campaign for the **BriefKit** content summarizer. The tone is highly professional, concise, intelligent, and focused on showing deep respect for the reader's time and inbox.

---

**Subject:** Welcome to BriefKit: Your first summary is ready
**Preheader:** We respect your time. Let’s start converting long-form reports into 30-second breakthroughs.

**Body:**

Hello [First Name],

Welcome to **BriefKit**.

You have joined a community of busy executives, researchers, and professional curators who have decided to break the "skim cycle" and reclaim their attention. 

We believe your reading list shouldn’t feel like a second job. Your account is active on our **Free Plan**, which includes 5 high-fidelity summaries every single month.

### Your First Step:
To see how much time you can save, summarize your first article today:
1. **Go to your workspace:** [Access Dashboard](https://briefkit.co/dashboard)
2. **Drop in a link:** Paste any URL, email newsletter, or PDF report.
3. **Get the signal:** Watch BriefKit distill it into a clean, 30-second brief containing the core arguments and key data points.

No noise. No fluff. Just the insight.

[ **Summarize My First Article →** ](https://briefkit.co/dashboard)

Thank you for choosing BriefKit. We respect your time, and we're here to help you protect it.

Sincerely,

— **The BriefKit Team**
