## 2. Key Features (We Respect Your Time)
*Focused on high-performance utility without unnecessary fluff.*

### Feature 1: Deep Semantic Parsing (The Signal, Not the Noise)
- **Description:**
  BriefKit doesn't just shorten text—it understands context. Our semantic engine extracts critical data, core arguments, and action items while filtering out fluff, introductions, and repetitive phrasing. Get raw intellectual value in seconds.

### Feature 2: Multi-Source Ingestion (Read Wherever You Work)
- **Description:**
  Whether it’s a PDF report, a locked newsletter, or a 5,000-word industry analysis, BriefKit ingests it effortlessly. Send articles directly from your browser extension, paste URLs, or upload documents to receive an instant, perfectly structured brief.

### Feature 3: Personalized Digest Engine (Your Knowledge, Curated)
- **Description:**
  Organize your summaries into custom collections or let BriefKit compile them into a streamlined weekly digest. Tailor your summaries to focus on specific themes like market trends, technical execution, or executive decisions.
