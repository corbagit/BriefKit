# BriefKit Pricing & Plan Descriptions

This document outlines the detailed value propositions, subscription terms, and user benefit messaging for all **BriefKit** pricing plans. It serves as a single source of truth for pricing-related copy on the landing page, user dashboard, checkout flows, and system emails.

---

## 1. Pricing Strategy & Value Proposition
BriefKit's pricing structure is designed to minimize friction for new users while scaling seamlessly with their volume of execution:
- **Starter ($0)** acts as an interactive playground. It allows creators to experience the "Brief to Breakthrough" moment without any initial investment or credit card required.
- **Pro ($29/month)** is the sweet spot for professional marketers, active creators, and small product teams. It unlocks the true superpower of BriefKit—unlimited briefs, AI-driven refinement, and native exports to modern collaboration suites.
- **Team ($79/month)** is targeted directly at digital agencies and cross-functional product departments. It delivers enterprise-grade flexibility through custom template libraries, unlimited team seats, and direct API access.

## 5. Billing & Pricing FAQs

- **How do I switch between plans?**
  You can upgrade, downgrade, or change your billing parameters at any time from your **Settings** tab. When upgrading to Pro or Team, your billing cycle restarts immediately, and any unused portion of a previous paid subscription is automatically prorated.

- **What happens if I cancel my Pro subscription?**
  Upon cancellation, you will retain access to your Pro features and unlimited briefs until the end of your current billing period. Afterward, your account will revert to the Starter tier, and your existing files will remain viewable but locked to further edits if they exceed Starter limits.

- **Are there any hidden fees or seat charges on the Team plan?**
  None. The Team plan is a flat $79/month subscription. Whether you have 3 team members or 30, your price remains exactly the same. We believe agencies should scale their output, not their software bills.

- **Can I pay with PayPal?**
  Yes. During checkout, you can select either Stripe or PayPal. Both support secure, automated monthly renewals and give you instant access to your new tier upon checkout completion.
