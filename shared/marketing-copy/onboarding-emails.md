All templates are designed to fit clean, modern HTML formats that look beautiful on both desktop and mobile clients.

- Meet your new strategic Co-Pilot

**Preheader Text:**
Discover how BriefKit’s Smart Iteration keeps your timelines and drafts synchronized when client specs shift.

**Body Copy:**

Hi [First Name],

We hope you've had a chance to build your first kit!

In traditional marketing and product workflows, when client requirements or product specs change, everything breaks down. You have to update the strategy deck, rewrite the copywriting drafts, edit the task boards, and hold another alignment meeting.

But with BriefKit, your brief is **active source code**.

We’d love to introduce you to our favorite superpower: **Smart Iteration**.

1. Open any of your existing assembled kits.
2. Select **Edit Brief** or chat directly with your conversational **AI Co-Pilot** (e.g., *"Change our target audience from busy remote workers to busy parents"* or *"Add a technical API integration phase to the timeline checklist"*).
3. Watch the magic happen. In real time, BriefKit re-evaluates and updates your checklists, adjusts your messaging guidelines, and adapts your copywriting drafts.

No out-of-date files. No alignment drift. Just pure, continuous momentum.
