## 7. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
*Visual style: Accordion layout with clean borders.*

- **Q: What formats of briefs do you support?**
  **A:** You can paste plain text, bullet points, raw conversation transcripts, or upload complete PDF files. Our parser is designed to extract meaning from both structured briefs and messy brain dumps.

- **Q: Does the AI write the actual deliverables or just organize them?**
  **A:** Both. BriefKit structures the project timelines and actionable checklists, and it also generates drafts of copy, email templates, and social posts based on the messaging strategy defined in your brief.

- **Q: How does Smart Iteration work?**
  **A:** When you modify any section of your input brief (such as changing a target audience or adding a new launch channel), our engine re-evaluates the entire kit. It updates your checklists, adapts your copy, and shifts your strategic timeline in real time so your assets never become outdated.

- **Q: Can we export to tools we already use?**
  **A:** Yes. We currently support full exports to Notion (with custom dashboard templates), Markdown, Slack channels, and PDF formats. We are continuously adding new integrations.

- **Q: Is my client data secure?**
  **A:** Absolutely. Your briefs, plans, and files are encrypted both in transit and at rest. We never use your proprietary client data to train public models.
